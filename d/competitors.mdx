## Add Competitors Manually

Navigate to the Competitors page and use the Add Competitor form:

![Screenshot 2025-07-18 at 20.47.22.png](/images/Screenshot2025-07-18at20.47.22.png)

- **Name:** Enter the competitor's brand name as it typically appears in AI responses
- **URL:** Add their main website domain (used to detect source citations)
- Click **Add Competitor** to save
